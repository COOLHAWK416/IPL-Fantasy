HOW IT WORKS
From league creation to live scoring in 4 steps
01
🏆
Create Your League
Set your budget, squad size, and league type. Invite friends via a shareable link. Choose H2H or classic points format.
02
🎯
Live Draft Auction
Every manager bids in real-time. 60 seconds per player across 3 rounds — Capped Indians, Uncapped Indians, and Overseas.
03
⚡
Set Your XI
Pick your Starting XI before each match. Assign a Captain (2×) and Vice-Captain (1.5×). Make transfers via waiver wire.
04
📊
Score Points Live
Points sync automatically from live IPL scorecards. Runs, wickets, catches, strike rates — full IPL fantasy scoring rules.
